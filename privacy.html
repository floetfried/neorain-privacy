No Data   Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Manuel Huebner built NeoRain as a free live wallpaper app. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

Information Collection and Use

NeoRain does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device and has no internet communication capabilities.

Permissions

NeoRain requests the SET_WALLPAPER permission solely to allow you to set the Matrix rain animation as your live wallpaper. This permission is used only when you explicitly choose to set the wallpaper and grants access to no personal data.

Third-Party Services

NeoRain does not use any third-party services, analytics, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools.

Children's Privacy

NeoRain does not collect any personally identifiable information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Any updates will be posted on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for changes.
